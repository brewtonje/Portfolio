About Jamie

Jamie Brewton is a video artist. Her current practice focuses the intersection of art, technology, and society. Jamie is origionally from Houston, Texas and Jakarta, Indonesia. Identifying as multi-racial, some of her art discusses identiy and what it means to be a "Third Culutre Kid amongst other activism."
